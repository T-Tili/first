About us

At Skyland Tourism Limited Sirketi, we are committed to provide luxurious travel services to our valued clients while promoting one of the world’s most unique and desirable destinations in Turkey.

We are a one stop travel management agency governed by world class talents and experiences in offering excellent healthcare tourism and other travel services including ticket operation services, processing of visas, as well as education, investment, work and residence permits, accommodation arrangement and car rental.

We pride ourselves in the delivery of high quality, professional services that are beyond clients’ expectations, within the framework of quality, responsibility, equity and sustainable development. By capitalizing on our knowledge and expertise of the local operating environment, we deliver global best practices, standards and systems that offer our clients best value. We strive to fulfill our objectives tailored to our clients’ needs whilst complying with legal requirements, regulation and company’s procedures.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver top notch travel services with readiness and promptness with the goal of ensuring stress-free travels in addition to lead investors to successful business ventures.

 

 

Vision

To establish our mark as the leading tourism company providing quality, creative, innovative, competitive and socially responsible services in the region and also be the reliable source for new and profitable business opportunities.

 

Values

Our core values are:

  • Prices
  • Responsibility
  • Integrity
  • Customer Focus
  • Excellence
  • Service orientation
