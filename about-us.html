Management Team

Zeina Waheed - Managing Director/CEO

Zeina is an entrepreneur and Business Consultant with vast experience and interest in diverse economic sectors, including Travels and Tourism, Events Planning, Beauty & Pageantry, Security, Commodity Trading, Construction and Heavy Equipment. She is a highly competent business facilitator and promoter who has been responsible for market entry and product launch for many international companies. Utilizing her managerial skills and vast business networks in various countries (notably, Holland, Turkey, Australia, UAE, Lebanon and Nigeria), she is able to arrange cooperation agreements, commercial contracts, and investments with government institutions and private sector companies towards achieving corporate goals.

R. Nihan Demir – Executive Director, Business Development

Nihan is a multilingual hotel and travels management expert with vast knowledge and experience in tourism and event management. She also has experience in Human Resource management and Interior Designs. She leads the planning, development, marketing and execution of all the Company’s engagements, including formation of enduring relationships with clientele. She personally negotiated recent contracts which resulted in securing various lucrative projects in new markets and refocused our marketing strategy for development of niche markets.

Ekong Etim – Director, Legal and Regulatory Services

Ekong is a specialist in International Commercial Law with varied practice experience garnered from private legal practice and working in government institution. In recent times, his practice has focused on engagements for Infrastructure Development, Concessions, Privatization, and Public-Private Partnerships. He has advised various governments, public enterprise managers, and investors on diverse legal issues involving transaction structures, legal and regulatory frameworks, project finance, joint ventures, and acquisitions. He has extensive experience in public-sector reforms and privatization, having acted as transaction advisor to the Bureau of Public Enterprises under the World Bank Privatization Assistance Program (PAP); overseeing the implementation of reform and privatization transactions, including providing high-level support to key sector leaders.
