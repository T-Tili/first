Investment Services

International investment can be a tricky endeavor- from language barrier and currency conversions to forcing exchanges and regulations. However, most financial advisors recommend it. The one thing most economists and financial advisors can agree upon is that Turkey is an attractive investment destination given their strong economic growth rates, which makes them very valuable to international investors.

To encourage strong investment ties with Turkey, Skyland offers advanced solutions to the tricks involved in investment. We assist in the business analytics of the investment, develop well thought-out investment strategies and prepare informative analysis reports

Business Analytics

This is a multi-stage process of evaluating an investment for profitability and risk. It ultimately has the purpose of measuring how the given investment is a good fit for a portfolio.

The first factor evaluated in any investment analysis is risk.

The second factor is the cash flows. This is considered to be essential as an investor will want to see if they can repay the investment while also repaying the assumed value of the risk on the investment.

The third factor of investment analysis is resale value. Profit from resale is made through a gain in the market value of the asset

Investment Strategies

Our aim in investment strategies is to capitalize on the continuance of existing trends in the market. Turkey is a fast-changing country in terms of trends in real estate investment market. Therefore, we develop our strategies partly on the government’s trend and partly on the size of the investment that leads us to two kinds- short term and long term investment strategies.

We set realistic expectations, put details in comparison, calculate risks and value increase and also offer more than one product option. Every strategy we develop also contains an exit plan of the asset which makes our services periscopic.
