Our Team

Ekong Etim – Director, Legal and Regulatory Services

Ekong is a specialist in International Commercial Law with varied practice experience garnered from private legal practice and working in government institution. In recent times, his practice has focused on engagements for Infrastructure Development, Concessions, Privatization, and Public-Private Partnerships. He has advised various governments, public enterprise managers, and investors on diverse legal issues involving transaction structures, legal and regulatory frameworks, project finance, joint ventures, and acquisitions. He has extensive experience in public-sector reforms and privatization, having acted as transaction advisor to the Bureau of Public Enterprises under the World Bank Privatization Assistance Program (PAP); overseeing the implementation of reform and privatization transactions, including providing high-level support to key sector leaders.
